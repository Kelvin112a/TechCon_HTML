Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in a university auditorium. What started as a local meetup has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of technological innovation, hosting early discussions on artificial intelligence, blockchain, quantum computing, and sustainable tech. Each year, we've expanded our reach and impact, consistently pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. Our mission is threefold:

  • Educate: Provide cutting-edge knowledge and skills to tech professionals
  • Connect: Foster meaningful connections between innovators, investors, and industry leaders
  • Inspire: Spark innovation that addresses real-world challenges

We believe that technology should be accessible, ethical, and transformative. TechCon 2024 continues this mission by focusing on sustainable innovation and inclusive technology development.
